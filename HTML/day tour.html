Buddhist pilgrimage Tour (5 Day)

Sri Lanka's state religion is Theravada Buddhism, which is practiced by 70.2 percent of the country's population. Since the advent of Buddhism in the third century BCE, the island has been a center of Buddhist research and learning, producing renowned academics like as Buddhaghosa and maintaining the enormous Pli Canon. Sri Lankan kings have played a key role in the preservation and restoration of the island's Buddhist institutions for much of its history.

Hike To Adems Peak(Two day)

Make the trip to Adam's Peak and take in the breathtaking sunrise views across Sri Lanka's beautiful hill region. When you join us on this trip, you will face the heights of Adam's Peak, an important place of devotion. For ages, this towering mountain has piqued people's interest and served as a pilgrimage destination for more than 1000 years. Buddhists in Sri Lanka believe that it houses the holy imprint of Gautama Buddha, as well as the adobe of the deity "Sumana Saman," who guards the sacred footprint of Gautama Buddha. This challenging hiking tour will take you up Adam's Peak's most revered 5500 stairs.You can participate in this hiking excursion during the season or during the off-season. If you travel during the summer, there's a good possibility you'll see pilgrims performing centuries-old rites associated with Adam's Peak's past. In Sri Lanka, this is a must-see location!

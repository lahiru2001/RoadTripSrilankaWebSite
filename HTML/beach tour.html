Arugam Bay Tour

The Arugam Bay surf beach is located 314 kilometers south of Colombo and 212 kilometers south of the tiny town of Pottuvil in the Ampara District of Sri Lanka's Dry Zone on the country's south-east coast. Arugam Bay, a moon-shaped curl of soft sand, is home to a well-known point break, which many consider to be the greatest in the country. It's a little town with a few hundred residents, and everything is strewn over a single road that runs parallel to the ocean. In other words, it's the essence of the laid-back beach lifestyle that brought surfers and sunbathers to Sri Lanka in the first place.
